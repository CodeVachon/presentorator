Breaking the Monolith

Moving to a microservice based architecture

Paul Osman

  • Monolith: Large, all encompusing systems
  • Pros of Monoliths
    • Easy to Picture
    • Few Moving Parts
    • 1 Stack
  • Cons of Monoliths
    • Scaling is an "All or Nothing" solution
