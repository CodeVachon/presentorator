Upgrading the Ma and Pa Shop

a guide to introducing new processes to a growing business

The Scenario

  • a small company (less then 20 people) grows to a medium business with over 50 people.
  • the company is comprised of several products with supporting staff. Each product consisting of:
    • A product owner
    • Sales staff
    • Sales support staff
  • the company has 1 team of developers comprised of 5 people that is responsible for maintaining and developing all the companies digital products.
  • the company is extreamly resistant to change

Step 1

Introducing Agile

many companies maintaining and developing internal projects use a waterfall approch to development.

  • Pros to a Waterfall approch
    • Company knows what they are getting before the project starts
    • Company can chose to outsource that project
  • Cons to a Waterfall approch
    • Extreamly slow for development
    • Unflexable for scope creep or changes in priorities
  • Internal projects should be handled with an Agile procress

Agile Processes

Several agile processes exists to help companies develope software

  • Scrum
  • KanBan
  • Lean Software Development
